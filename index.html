About Me

My Journey

When I was a kid, I developed an interest in technology when I often modded video games for fun. This evolved into discovering my passion for programming during my first computer science course in high school. Since then, I have been constantly learning and building projects to improve my skills.

I am currently pursuing a B.A degree in Computer Science from Macalester College, where I focused on software engineering and artificial intelligence. During my studies, I completed internships at Gumbo where I was able to apply my knowledge into a real world setting in the medical field.

Outside of coding, I enjoy learning new languages, reading, and very much into music. I am currently learning the electric guitar and music production.

What I Value

Clean Code: I believe in writing maintainable, well-documented code that other developers can easily understand and build upon.

User Experience: Every line of code I write is with the end user in mind, ensuring intuitive interfaces and smooth interactions.

Continuous Learning: Technology evolves rapidly, and I'm committed to staying current with the latest tools, frameworks, and best practices.

Collaboration: I thrive in team environments where ideas are shared freely and everyone works together toward common goals.
